about us

Building Landmarks Since 1999

At Creissant BuildPro Pvt Ltd, based in Hosur and established in 1999, we are dedicated to delivering top-tier construction solutions that stand the test of time. Specializing solely in construction, we focus on building robust residential, industrial, and commercial spaces tailored to evolving needs.

Our core strength lies in residential construction, where we bring comfort, design precision, and functionality into every home we build. In the industrial and commercial sectors, we construct high-performance infrastructures that enable growth, productivity, and long-term value.

With over two decades of experience, our legacy is built on quality craftsmanship, on-time execution, and a deep understanding of the local landscape. Whether it’s a family home, a retail hub, or a large-scale factory, every project reflects our commitment to excellence, durability, and trust.
